# Sources

Raw CSV seed data loaded with `dbt seed` into the `grocery_ops_demo_sources` Snowflake schema.

* [raw_customers](raw_customers.md) — 30 customer accounts with loyalty tier and acquisition channel
* [raw_products](raw_products.md) — 25 product SKUs with cost and price in pence
* [raw_orders](raw_orders.md) — 40 orders spanning the full fulfilment lifecycle
* [raw_order_items](raw_order_items.md) — 94 line items linking orders to products
* [raw_fulfilment_centres](raw_fulfilment_centres.md) — 6 automated CFCs with capacity and robot fleet data
